Overview

Give a high-level overview of what you implemented in this homework. Think about what you've built as a whole. Share your thoughts on what interesting things you've learned from completing the homework. In this homework, we implemented a simple simulation by approximating surfaces as a combination of point masses and springs with Verlet Integration. To increase the realism of the simulation, we incorporated object collision, self-collision, and maximum spring lengths. We finally implemented some shared for diffusion shading, Blinn-Phong shading, texture mapping, bump/displacement shading, and environment shading. I personally found the simplification/approximation of surfaces and meshes as point masses and springs to be surprisingly effective and interesting.
